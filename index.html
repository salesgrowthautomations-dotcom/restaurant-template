About Us

Located at Navabharath Circle, Hampankatta, Café Flourista is known for its cozy atmosphere, friendly staff, and delicious menu. Popular dishes include the Mexican Fajita Bowl, Nutella French Toast, Shakshouka, and Raspberry Cold Brew Coffee.
